About Beatmap

What is Beatmap?

Beatmap is my answer to the overwhelming number of music and music services that are out there. I wanted something that helps you find good music, and gives you the freedom to listen using what you want. This is a work in progress on that idea.

How does it work?

All Beatmap needs is an artist that you like, and it will give you a huge list of new albums to listen to. First, it uses Last.fm to get artist recommendations based on its massive amount of user listening history. Then it starts grabbing albums and looks them up for you on Spotify and YouTube. Finally, it lets you listen, make playlists, and get information on these albums and artists.

What's next?

I am constantly working to improve and expand Beatmap. I am planning on integrating more popular music services, such as Streamus, Google Play Music, and Pandora. Also planned is for users to be able like and dislike albums, make and share playlists, and more. In the works is a scraping tool for RateYourMusic, which would provide more information on albums and their popularity. In the future, I plan to implement my own recommendation algorithm that would combine information from multiple services to provide even more accurate results.

If you like this project and have ideas to improve it, please let me know!
